About Document Embeddings

What are document embeddings?

Document embeddings are numerical vector representations of text that capture semantic meaning. This tool uses TF-IDF vectorization to convert your document text into embeddings.

Supported file types:

  • PDF documents
  • Excel spreadsheets (.xlsx, .xls)
  • Word documents (.docx)

What can you do with embeddings?

Embeddings are useful for:

  • Document similarity comparisons
  • Semantic search systems
  • Text clustering and classification
  • Building recommendation systems
